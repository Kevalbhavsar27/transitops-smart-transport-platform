Upload Guidelines

Follow these recommendations to keep vehicle documents organised and valid.

  • Supported Files

    Upload PDF, JPG, JPEG or PNG documents only.

  • Maximum File Size

    Each uploaded document must be 5 MB or smaller.

  • Correct Vehicle

    Confirm that the document belongs to the selected vehicle.

  • Expiry Information

    Add the correct expiry date for insurance, permits and certificates.

  • Readable Document

    Ensure the file is clear, complete and easy to read.

Vehicle documents are stored for authorised TransitOps users and operational compliance.
