Trip Planning Guide

Follow these checks to create a safe and accurate fleet trip.

  • Verify the Route

    Confirm both pickup and destination locations before saving.

  • Check Vehicle Capacity

    Cargo weight must not exceed the selected vehicle's maximum load.

  • Driver Eligibility

    Assign an available driver with a valid and suitable licence.

  • Accurate Distance

    Planned distance supports fuel, cost and performance calculations.

  • Review Revenue

    Enter the expected customer payment or trip income accurately.

The server will validate vehicle availability, driver availability, licence validity and cargo capacity when the trip is saved or dispatched.
