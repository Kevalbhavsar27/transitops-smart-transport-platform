Driver Registration Guide

Follow these checks to maintain safe and compliant fleet operations.

  • Verify Licence Number

    Enter the number exactly as printed on the official driving licence.

  • Check Expiry Date

    Drivers with expired licences cannot be dispatched on trips.

  • Correct Category

    Ensure the licence category is suitable for the assigned vehicle type.

  • Safety Score

    Use a consistent safety rating process for all registered drivers.

  • Availability Status

    Only available drivers should appear during trip dispatch.

Suspended, on-trip or expired-licence drivers must not be assigned to another trip.
